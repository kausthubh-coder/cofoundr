Stop Trying to Build Alone.

Most student founders get stuck before they ever start — not because they lack ideas, but because they're trying to do everything themselves.

Random networking produces random results. LinkedIn, Discord, and college events are noisy and produce mismatched partnerships that fall apart fast. There is no filter for vision alignment or commitment level.

Skill gaps kill momentum. Solo founders stall on what they can't do — engineering, design, sales — and never ship. Momentum dies when you hit a wall you can't climb alone.

Most student startups never make money because they're treated as experiments. The wrong cofounder accelerates this failure.

Cofoundr is the curated platform that matches serious student founders with the right cofounder — so they stop trying to build alone and start building companies that actually make money.
